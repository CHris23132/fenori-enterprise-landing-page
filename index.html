Our vision

Humans in control, AI doing the heavy lifting.

Legal, HR and compliance teams bring nuance, empathy and judgment. Fenori brings speed, recall and structure. Together, they redefine how organizations understand risk, support their people and meet their obligations.

The result? Less time chasing documents, more time advising the business, closing matters and supporting employees.
